About the interactive web app: Literature of Intelligent Water Information Extraction Using AI

This is the accompany interactive web app for the review paper (invited feature paper), titled "Towards Synoptic Water Monitoring Systems: A Review of AI Methods for Automating Water Body Detection and Water Quality Monitoring Using Remote Sensing", and submitted to the special issue (Advances in the Monitoring, Diagnosis, and Optimisation of Water Systems) of the open access journal Sensors.
